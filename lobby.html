Lobby

What is it?

Lobby was set up by myself as a collection of discord communities for gamers. This will hopefully provide you with people to play with in an attempt to better enhance your gaming experience. This collection is based off of public servers- ranging from small community-run servers, all the way up to large LFT servers or content-creator run discords. Please use the blue button to access lobby. If you have any suggestions of communities you'd like me to add, feel free to learn how to message me with the button below.
